Privacy guarantees

No data leaves your device

Your recordings, audio files, and analysis remain on your machine. The app does not send any information to servers, and it does not retain personal data.

Offline-only functionality

Everything is designed to work without an internet connection once the site is loaded.

No user tracking

There are no analytics, no tracking scripts, and no data collection mechanisms.

Local review and control

You decide when and how to use the app; no external service has access to your audio.

Your data is your own

We believe privacy is fundamental. This app is built from the ground up to keep your voice data private. No account is required, no cloud storage is used, and no information is ever collected or retained.

No account required

Use the app without creating an account or logging in. Pure anonymity by design.

No data transmission

Your audio never leaves your computer. All processing occurs locally on your device.

No cookies or analytics

We don't track your usage, preferences, or behavior. You are in complete control.
