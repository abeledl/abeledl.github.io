What this app does

Extracts audio formants and shows their position in a vowel space

Record or upload audio, then see where the extracted formants land in a vowel chart. This is ideal for linguistics practice, phonetics exploration, and private acoustic analysis.

Audio formant extraction

Analyze vowel sounds and extract formant frequencies from recordings or audio files.

Vowel space mapping

Visualize formant values in a classic vowel space layout for quick comparison and insight.

Offline processing

All computation happens locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored remotely.
